Places to Visit in Ajmer

Ajmer is a beautiful and religious city located in Rajasthan. There are various touristy places to visit in Ajmer like Dargah of Khwaja Muin-ud-din Chishti, which is the shrine of Sufi Saint Moinuddin Chishti. This is the grave of the revered saint and sees a pouring in of devotees of all faiths. No less well known is the Savitri Temple, which is an ancient temple high up the Ratnagiri hills at Pushkar and is very popular among tourists and devotees. The other popular places to visit in Ajmer are Brahma Temple, Shri Rangnath Swami Temple and Abdullah Khans Tomb to name a few.
