Places to Visit in Udaipur

There are many historic places to visit in Udaipur. In fact, most of the Udaipur places to visit are synonymous with the numerous forts and palaces that unravel the history of the destination. These spectacular structures have mind-blowing architecture and are stunning to look at. The City Palace is perhaps the most stunning of the lot and is one of the biggest palaces in Rajasthan. There are several other historic attractions in Udaipur like Bagore Ki Haveli, Lake Palace and Monsoon Palace. Another of the interesting places for sightseeing in Udaipur, besides forts and palaces is the Vintage Car Museum, which houses the car collection of the Maharaja of Udaipur.
